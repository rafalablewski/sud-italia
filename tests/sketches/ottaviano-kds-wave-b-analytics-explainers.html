2 · The ⓘ explainer sheet (Rule #12 — five sections, fixed order)

Profit margin

Share of every złoty of revenue left after food + labour + overhead — the headline efficiency number.

INSTITUTIONAL ANALYSIS
A healthy quick-service pizza operation runs a 12–18% net margin; below 8% is a watch-flag, above 20% is top-decile. Investors gate follow-on capital on margin trend, not just revenue growth — a chain growing revenue while margin slips is buying sales with discounts.
IN PLAIN TERMS
On a 9 800 zł day at 15% margin you keep about 1 470 zł after everything. Push margin two points to 17% and that same day keeps 1 666 zł — roughly an extra 70 000 zł a year per location, with no new customers.
TIPS — HOW TO PUSH THIS LEVER
Trim the bottom-quartile dishes by food cost (see Menu engineering). Tighten portioning on cheese + cured meats. Shift labour to match the slot forecast. Move guests to higher-margin channels with a dine-in upsell.
METHODOLOGY — HOW THIS IS DETERMINED
margin = totalProfit ÷ totalRevenue, where profit = revenue − (food cost + labour + allocated overhead) over the selected range. Source: GET /api/v1/admin/summary (profitMargin), computed chain-wide from settled orders.
