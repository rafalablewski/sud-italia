Brace & Basil

A warm-dark re-skin for the kitchen display. The wall trades cool near-black and magenta-red for the glow of the forno — warm espresso surfaces, an ember accent, fresh basil for done — so tickets read at a glance under bright kitchen glare and the timing ramp tells a whole story by colour alone. Drop-in over the Core dark tokens → flows to the Swift palette via gen:native.

01Surfaces & ink — warm, not clinical

The wall canvas warms from #100f12 (cool) to #15110D (espresso). Same depth, but it reads as a calm room, not a dead screen — and food on the line looks appetising, not grey. Ink is warm cream for a 12-hour shift.
