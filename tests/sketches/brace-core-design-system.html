Brace & Basil

One warm-dark identity across the whole Core — POS, the floor, the kitchen wall, the guest flow. The glow of the forno replaces cool black-and-red: warm espresso surfaces, an ember accent, fresh basil, a touch of saffron. Food looks appetising, status reads at a glance, and it holds up in a bright kitchen and a candle-lit room. Toggle Light top-right — the kiosk wall stays dark regardless.
