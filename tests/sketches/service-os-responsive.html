Same components

Command bar, lens rail, Context Dock, tiles, tickets and tender sheet are one component set — they don't fork per device, they reflow. Parity is structural, not two codebases.

Thumb-first on phone

The lens rail drops to a bottom tab bar; the Context Dock becomes a bottom sheet above it. Everything a server needs sits in the lower third where the thumb lives.

Density adapts

Web shows all four KDS lanes at once; phone turns them into a swipeable carousel. Floor tiles go 4-up → 2-up. Nothing is hidden — it re-lays out.

Guest joins the same stream

The guest phone is the fourth renderer of the same check over one SSE stream — identical timing + status the staff app and kitchen wall see.
