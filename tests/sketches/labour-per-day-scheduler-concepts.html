Weekly shift scheduler — 5 concepts

Restaurant open 7 days · staff work 5–6 · different hours per day · ≥12 h rest between shifts. Hours + salary (Labour) and the coverage grid (Shift plan) recompute from the schedule.
Model shift
Each person gets a 7-day week: a shift (or off) per day. hrs/wk = Σ daily shift lengths.
Rest rule
< 12 h rest flagged when a shift starts less than 12 h after the previous day's end (incl. Sun→Mon wrap).
Coverage
Shift plan grid gains day tabs (Mon–Sun) — the demand curve + line-cover check run per day.

  • Shift templates (03) layered on top as an accelerator: pick a template per cell, or type custom hours.

  • A standing rest < 12 h check across the week (incl. the Sun→Mon wrap) surfaces on the offending cell and as a card-level count.
