CRM — the chosen direction
a customer book that reads as one system: Regulars

Regulars is the CRM direction we're taking forward. It reuses the POS + KDS visual language — the same Inter + JetBrains Mono type, the Atlas dark canvas, the tone palette and the header/footer chrome — so the till, the line and the customer book all read as one system. The thesis: a food truck lives on its regulars, so the screen is a searchable customer book on the left and a deep relationship profile on the right — lifetime value, an RFM health score that cools toward churn, order history, loyalty tier, contact consent, notes, and an AI next-best-action for every customer.
