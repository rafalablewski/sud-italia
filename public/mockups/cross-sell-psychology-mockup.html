Audit §3 · Cross-sell psychology

Bundles, pairing graph, segmented delivery, Teams

Four moves on the same drawer + one new public page. Each one expands an existing primitive — the family wallet becomes Teams, the chips graph becomes contextual, the combo banner becomes a tier ladder, the threshold copy becomes segmented.

3.1 Pairing graph — the why

CROSS_SELL_MAP stays as the safety net, but each candidate gets a per-cart score from four signals. Same chips you ship today, ranked smarter:

  • MMargin × attach. Espresso (83% GM × 60% attach) keeps its first-place anchor.
  • HHour bias. Espresso 0.82 at 11:00, 0.31 at 19:00. Banner copy already pivots; chips now do too.
  • CThis customer. Espresso last 4 orders → boost. Never tried → small novelty bonus.
  • NNovelty decay. The thing they always add gets quiet; chip rotates to the next-best earner.
