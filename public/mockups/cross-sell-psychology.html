Audit §3.1 – 3.4

Cross-sell psychology — visual draft

Four new behaviours layered into the same drawer that ships cart-upsell-v2. Same tokens, fonts, icons and copy patterns as CartDrawer.tsx — just augmented with a gated bundle ladder, smarter chip ranking, segmented delivery copy, and a corporate-order banner when the active wallet is a Sud Italia Corporate account.

Toggle the controls below. Hour switcher gates the lunch ladder (only renders 11–14); cart-quantity switcher gates the Family Feast ladder (renders at ≥5 mains, hint at 3–4); segment switcher repaints the delivery target; corporate toggle adds the gold/green banner with the Sud Italia Corporate kicker.

What's new — full §3 coverage

Cross-sell deltas vs the v2 cart

  • 1 Contextual chip ranking + brand-voice reasons. §3.1. Same three-up grid + priority-driven order (espresso → tiramisù → drink). Composite score (margin × hour-bias × per-customer attach × novelty decay) resolves ties within a priority bucket. Reason copy pivots to “you added it 3 of last 4 visits” when the signal is strong; otherwise the brand-voice override table below wins.
  • 2 Gated bundle ladder above the chips. §3.2. Lunch ladder shows only during the lunch window (default 11–14, admin-configurable). Family Feast shows only when the cart has ≥5 mains; at 3 or 4 mains the customer sees a one-line nudge instead of the full ladder (“Add 1 more pizza or pasta to unlock Family Feast”). Most picked on the default tier (red ribbon), Best value on the anchor (gold ribbon), decoy slightly muted.
  • 3 Segmented free-delivery threshold. §3.3. Four bands instead of three: first-time 39 zł, growing 2–4 → 49 zł, regular 5+ → 59 zł, Gold/Platinum free. The “tuned for you” annotation surfaces the personalisation so customers don’t think the bar moved by accident.
  • 4 Sud Italia Corporate banner. §3.4. Renders above the ToD banner when the active wallet has a corporate config. Floor is 6 employees (“>5” from the brief, enforced at promotion). Employees see "Ordering with Acme · billed to the company card", head sees head-bonus accrual. Public landing at /corporate/[slug]; admin at /admin/corporate.

Q: where it plugs in

Same files, additive surfaces

  • upsell.ts — scorePairing() + PairingContext; ITEM_REASON_OVERRIDES map (espresso → "Never too late", tiramisù → "Pizzaiolo's fav", burrata/bruschetta → "Freshly-baked today").
  • bundles.ts (new) — DEFAULT_BUNDLES + DEFAULT_BUNDLE_RULES + resolveBundleAvailability() (returns show / hint / hidden).
  • BundleLadder — re-resolves availability on a one-minute tick so the lunch ladder appears and disappears with the local clock; surfaces the family hint when within 2 mains of the threshold.
  • checkout API — re-validates the bundle composition and sends one Stripe line at the locked price with the items itemized.
  • FamilyWallet — optional corporate config (slug, name, billingEmail, headBonusBps, minEmployees, autoPreorderDay/Time, locationSlug). Server enforces minEmployees ≥ 6.
  • /admin/upsell → Bundle availability — edit lunch hours + family quantity gate per location.
  • /admin/corporate (renamed from /admin/teams) — promote a wallet (≥6 members), edit config, remove.
Out of scope here

Deliberately not in this commit

  • Per-bundle composition editor in /admin/upsell — bundle slot definitions still ship via DEFAULT_BUNDLES + LocationUpsellConfig.bundles JSON; the schema editor ships next.
  • Monthly invoice cron for corporate heads — config persisted; cron lands in a follow-up.
  • Auto-pre-order reminder — schedule persisted; the cron that fires “Wednesday corporate lunch — 4 of 8 have ordered, 2h to go” lands next.
