Hero redesign — concept gallery

Sud Italia · V8 Trattoria

Three directions for the homepage hero, each built on the real V8 palette (parchment, oxblood, terracotta) and the Cormorant + Lora type pairing. All three lead with appetite and carry the live Ready in ~15 min chip — tying the hero to the operator-managed speed guarantee now wired through the cart.

What the critique asked for, and how these answer it
  • 11.4: “The hero is a dark gradient… the competitor is showing you a Margherita on a wood peel under sodium light.” → every variant puts a real pie front-and-centre.
  • 11.4: “‘Ready in 15 minutes’ promised, then no ETA before paying.” → the ETA chip rides in the hero and is now live in the cart before payment.
